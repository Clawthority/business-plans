## The Strategy: Portfolio Diversification

Don't bet on one product. Build many, let winners emerge.

**Why this works:**
1. Multiple revenue streams = lower risk
2. Cross-promotion between products
3. Shared infrastructure (same tech stack)
4. Learnings apply across all products
5. Some will fail, some will succeed
