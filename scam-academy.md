# 🎓 Scam Academy — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Scam Academy is a consumer scam prevention training platform that teaches people to recognize scams by practicing on realistic simulations. Think "KnowBe4 for regular people."

**Market:** $30B+ lost to scams annually in the US alone. $3.4B to elder fraud. The consumer scam training market is virtually unserved.

**Revenue Model:** Freemium SaaS — free for basic, $10-25/month for families, $500-5,000/year for schools/enterprise.

---

## Problem

- **$30B+/year** lost to scams in the US
- **$3.4B/year** lost to elder fraud alone
- **$5.6B/year** lost to crypto scams
- Existing solutions are reactive (report after the fact) or corporate-only (KnowBe4 = $4B, no consumer product)
- Families have NO tools to protect elderly parents
- Schools have no curriculum for digital safety

## Solution

A training platform that sends realistic scam simulations to users. If they fall for it, they get instant education. If they spot it, they get positive reinforcement. Difficulty adapts based on performance.

**12 simulation types:** Phishing, prize scams, romance scams, grandparent scams, crypto scams, tech support, IRS/tax scams.

**Key differentiator:** Every simulation is generated with randomized elements (companies, amounts, URLs). No two are the same.

## Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Users confused (real vs. simulated) | Clear "TRAINING" labels, opt-in only |
| Scam templates become outdated | Weekly trend monitoring + auto-updates |
| Low engagement | Gamification (streaks, achievements) |
| Privacy concerns | No personal data stored, GDPR compliant |
