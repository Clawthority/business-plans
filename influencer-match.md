# 🤝 Influencer Match — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

Connects micro-influencers (1K-50K followers) with affiliate and product offers. Automated matching engine for the $21B influencer marketing industry.

**Market:** $21B influencer marketing industry growing 30% YoY. Micro-influencers have better engagement but no access to deals.

---

## Problem

- **Micro-influencers** (1K-50K) have 60%+ better engagement than mega-influencers
- Brands are shifting 60%+ of budgets to micro-influencers
- But micro-influencers can't find deals (agencies only work with big accounts)
- Existing platforms (AspireIQ, Grin) cost $500-5,000/month — too expensive for small creators

## Solution

Automated platform that:
1. Finds micro-influencers by niche
2. Pulls affiliate offers from major networks
3. Matches them algorithmically
4. Alerts influencers about relevant deals

**Free for influencers.** Revenue from brand/affiliate side.

---

## Target Market

| Segment | Size | Revenue Potential |
|---------|------|------------------|
| Micro-influencers (1K-50K) | 50M+ globally | Free → premium |
| Brands seeking micro-influencers | 1M+ brands | Matching fees |

## Revenue Model

| Stream | How It Works | Potential |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Affiliate commissions | We connect influencers to offers, earn % | $100-10K/month |
| Brand matching fees | Brands pay to access influencer DB | $500-5K/month |
| Premium subscriptions | Influencers pay for deal alerts | $10-30/month |
| API licensing | Other platforms use our matching | $1K-10K/month |

---

## Competitive Advantage

- **Free for influencers** (competitors charge $500+/month)
- **Automated matching** (no manual searching)
- **Niche-specific** (fitness influencers get fitness offers)
- **Telegram-based** (no app to download)

## Go-to-Market

1. Seed database with influencers from Reddit/Twitter/YouTube
2. Curate high-commission affiliate offers (10+ niches, 50+ brands)
3. Launch free Telegram bot for influencers
4. Approach brands with "here are 1,000 micro-influencers in your niche"
5. Scale through influencer word-of-mouth (they share deals with friends)
