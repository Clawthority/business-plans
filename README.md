## Plan Structure

Every business plan follows the same framework:

1. **Executive Summary** — One-paragraph pitch
2. **Problem & Solution** — What pain, how we solve it
3. **Market Analysis** — TAM/SAM/SOM, competitors, positioning
4. **Revenue Model** — Pricing tiers, monetization strategy
5. **Go-to-Market** — Launch sequence, channels, first 100 users
6. **Unit Economics** — CAC, LTV, margins, breakeven
7. **12-Month Projections** — Month-by-month MRR targets
8. **Risks & Mitigations** — What could go wrong, how we'd adapt
