# 🛡️ Scam Shield — Business Plan

## Executive Summary
Multi-surface scam detection engine. Analyzes messages, URLs, crypto addresses, and phone numbers for 100+ scam patterns.

**Market:** $30B+ lost to scams annually. No unified detection tool for consumers.

---

## Problem
Current tools cover only one surface (Truecaller = phones, Norton = antivirus, Google = URLs). Nobody checks ALL surfaces.

## Solution
One tool that checks messages, links, crypto addresses, and phone numbers. 100+ patterns across 7 categories: urgency, financial, authority, romance, crypto, phishing, social engineering.
