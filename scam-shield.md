# 🛡️ Scam Shield — Business Plan

## Status: MVP Built, Zero Revenue, Zero Users

**What exists:** 100+ scam patterns, message/URL/crypto/phone analysis, tested and working.
**What doesn't exist:** Users, revenue, proof.

## What We Know
- $30B+ lost to scams annually
- No unified consumer scam detection tool
- Tests show it catches 70-80% of scam patterns

## Next Actions
1. Deploy as Telegram bot
2. Let people forward suspicious messages for instant analysis
3. Track: Do they come back? Do they share it?

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 10 people use it
- [ ] 3 people share results with friends
- [ ] 1 person says "this saved me"

## Revenue Approach
Free first. Charge when people ask for more (family protection, always-on monitoring).
