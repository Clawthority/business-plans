# 🧠 MindfulBot — Business Plan

## Executive Summary

MindfulBot is a screen addiction replacement system delivered via Telegram. Not a blocker — a better alternative. Built on peer-reviewed behavioral science.

**Market:** $280B mental health market. Screen addiction is the #1 entry point. 3.8B smartphone users globally.

**Revenue Model:** Freemium — free basic tracking, $5/month premium.

---

## Problem

- **3+ hours/day** average screen time globally
- Existing solutions (Screen Time, app blockers) rely on willpower — the thing people lack
- Meditation apps solve the wrong problem (people don't need to meditate more, they need to scroll less)
- Therapy costs $150-300/week
- Nobody provides an accountability partner for screen habits

## Solution

A Telegram bot that replaces scrolling with micro-challenges, streak tracking, and accountability — all based on behavioral science research.

**Science-backed features:**
- Replace routine, don't remove (Duhigg)
- Variable rewards (Schüll)
- Accountability partner (65% goal achievement — ASTD research)
- Implementation intentions (2-3x follow-through — Gollwitzer)
- Traction over focus (Nir Eyal)
